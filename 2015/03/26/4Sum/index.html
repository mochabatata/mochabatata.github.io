4Sum

Thinking and Analysis:

Use a hastable to store a every two numbers’ sum and its all possible pairs.
Same as three sum, use two nested loop to match the gap with all sums in the hashtable.
If matched, skip the all repeated elements and add them to result.

Runtime: 224 ms (Leetcode test)
